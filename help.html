We provide our families joyful moments that lead to lasting memories, and we are able to do this because of believers like you. Learn how you can support the Village at the links below.

Get Involved

Volunteer

The Village depends on volunteers to fulfill hundreds of volunteer shifts every day. Whether you're in the Central Florida area, interested in a volunteer vacation, or are an Alumni Family wanting to give back, apply today to become a part of our volunteer family.

Fundraise

Support the Village’s mission from anywhere in the world by hosting a fundraiser at school, in your community, with your company, or by creating your own online fundraiser.

Give

Generous individuals – just like you – help fulfill our mission. Please consider making a financial donation, an in-kind donation, or learning more about our planned giving opportunities.

Events & Promotions

Attend a fundraising event, participate in one of our partners’ promotions, or create your own online fundraiser to help support our mission.

Purchase with a Purpose

You can support the Village's mission by shopping for your favorite products. Preferred retail partners will donate a portion of your payment to Give Kids The World.

Company & Community Partnerships

Whether you are part of a company, social group, or organization, we will work with you to foster a mutually beneficial relationship.

Memory Market Gift Shop

Whether you are at the Village or browsing the store online, the Memory Market gift shop offers you the chance to take a little bit of the Village with you wherever you go. Memorabilia ranging from Village-themed apparel, jewelry, household items and much more is available on property and at the online store.
